Mom's Banana Bread Recipe

Delicious banana bread for a 9x5x3" Pan.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 3/4 Cups Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 Cup Whole Wheat Flour
  • 1 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 3 Large Bananas
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla
  • 1/2 Cup Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Beat together butter, vanilla, and sugar until fluffy.
  2. Beat in 1 egg at a time.
  3. Cobine flours, soda, & salt in a bowl; mix.
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet and mix well.
  5. Add bananas and mix well.
  6. Add chocolate chips.
  7. Place mixture into bread pan and bake for 45-55min at 350Â°F until poking a fork in the center comes out clean.
