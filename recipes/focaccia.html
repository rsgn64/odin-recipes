Focaccia Recipe

Super simple Focaccia for soup season.

Ingredients

    3/4 Cup Warm Water
    2 Tblsp Olive Oil
    2 Cups Flour
    1 Tblsp Sugar
    1 Tsp Salt
    1 1/2 Tsp Yeast
    Spice/Rosemary/Oil, etc for topping

Steps

    In a bread machine: Combine warm water, sugar and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 min (depending on yeast type) until the yeast has become very foamy.
    Add Oil, then Flour, then salt and run the dough cycle.
    Once dough cycle is complete, remove dough and flatten on a lightly oiled baking pan. Flatten to about a 12" circle. Cover and allow to rise until it's double size.
    Add topping(s) and then bake at 400Â°F for 15-18min.
