Mexican Black Beans Recipe

Black beans from dried done in the Instant Pot!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Onion Diced
  • 3 Cloves Garlic, Chopped
  • 1 Tsp Corriander
  • 1 Tsp Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 Tsp Chili Powder
  • 2 Cups Dried Black Beans
  • 3 Cups of water or broth.

Steps

  1. Saute Onion/Garlic in a bit of oil.
  2. Add spices and saute for a minute or two.
  3. Add water or broth and scrape the bottom.
  4. Add dried beans and then cook on High Pressure for 35min.
  5. Allow 20 minutes of natural pressure release.
